Objective

When designing and producing my artworks, I chose to let my imagination and creativity work out my design process to forge my own unique and interesting design in my illustrations. When working on something, I tend to design illustrations based on what interests me or how it relates to the theme that is being represented in the artworks. While designing, I like my work to be as simple as possible when working on the rough drafts before transitioning to include stronger elements in my artworks so that they can have a simplistic and bold statement. I like my artwork to be created for fun and exciting to look at, but I will give a strong expression of my ideas if I need to on certain topics if I want to. I like to make what I want to make and I want to find enjoyment and passion in my artworks that people would find interesting to look at and how I can find satisfaction in the joy they bring to people.

Unselfie

 This unselfie image is the result of my exercise where the objective of the assignment is to change the image of your selfie into a completely different form of image. When working on the image, I mess around with the options that were available to me and selecting random parts of my face to create an interesting and random pattern that ended up creating a glitchy techno theme image. The overall theme was a product of randomness that ended up creating an effective and amazing tech out gitch motif that produced spectacular results and popular reception.
